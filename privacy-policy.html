All local data is encrypted using AES-256 encryption, an industry-standard security protocol. The encryption key is stored in Android Keystore and protected by your device's security measures (PIN, pattern, fingerprint, or face recognition).

You can delete all local data at any time through Settings → Delete All Data. Uninstalling the app permanently removes all data from your device.
